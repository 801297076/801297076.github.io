Client Project Review 1

Reviewed by: Bogdan Gerasymenko

Project URL

https://webpages.charlotte.edu/padigwe/itis3135/project/clientproject/

The 616 Charlotte website does a great job introducing UNC Charlotte’s official Marvel fan club in a fun and inviting way. The homepage clearly explains the club’s purpose, emphasizing community, creativity, and a shared love for the Marvel universe. The design is visually appealing and fits the superhero theme well, while the content is clear, engaging, and informative. Features like event descriptions and thematic language help capture the spirit of the MCU. Overall, it’s a well-crafted student project that effectively promotes the club and invites students to get involved.
